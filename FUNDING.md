# SKYNZ Funding Strategy

Complete documentation of SKYNZ's bootstrap and Solana grant funding strategy.

---

## Executive Summary

SKYNZ uses a two-phase funding strategy: bootstrap for 6 months with personal capital ($5K-$10K), then apply for Solana Foundation grant ($25K-$50K) at month 3-4 when the platform shows traction. This approach maintains full control, avoids dilution, and reaches profitability by month 6.

---

## Phase 2: Solana Grant (Month 3-4)

### Solana Foundation Grants

**What Solana Offers**

The Solana Foundation provides grants to support ecosystem development:
- Creator economy projects
- Web3 platforms
- NFT projects
- Developer tools
- Community initiatives

**Grant Amounts:** $5K - $100K+
**Application Process:** Online form + review
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

### Why SKYNZ Qualifies

**Solana Foundation Mission Alignment**

SKYNZ aligns perfectly with Solana's mission:

1. **Creator Economy** — SKYNZ is built specifically for creators
2. **Web3 Integration** — Uses Solana blockchain for NFT certificates
3. **User Adoption** — Drives Solana wallet adoption (Phantom)
4. **Ecosystem Growth** — Builds on Metaplex standard
5. **Revenue Model** — Sustainable, not dependent on grants
6. **Community** — Builds creator community on Solana

### Grant Application Timeline

**Month 3: Prepare Application**

- Gather creator testimonials
- Show revenue metrics

**Month 3-4: Submit Application**
- Fill out Solana grant form

**Month 4: Grant Decision**

- If approved, receive funds

## Risk Mitigation

### Market Risk
**Risk:** Creators don't want to pay for pages
**Mitigation:** Blind survey before building; validate pricing; iterate based on feedback

### Execution Risk
**Risk:** Can't build MVP in time
**Mitigation:** Use Expo (managed framework); focus on MVP only; hire help if needed

### Funding Risk
**Risk:** Solana grant is rejected
**Mitigation:** Continue bootstrapping; apply to other grants; focus on profitability

### Competitive Risk
**Risk:** Competitors copy the model
**Mitigation:** First-mover advantage; network effect; community lock-in

### Regulatory Risk
**Risk:** Crypto regulation changes
**Mitigation:** Solana is established; use standard (Metaplex); consult legal
