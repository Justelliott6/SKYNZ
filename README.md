# SKYNZ — Creator-Owned Platform

**Live. Create. Connect.**

SKYNZ is a creator-owned platform where you own your page, prove it on blockchain, and earn from live chat, ads, and collectible badges—no platform lock-in, no algorithms controlling your content.

---

## Quick Overview

**What is SKYNZ?**

SKYNZ is the first creator-owned platform built on Solana blockchain. Creators buy limited-edition pages with NFT certificates proving ownership. Fans buy collectible badges linked to creator pages (like digital trading cards). Creators earn from ads, live chat, and fan engagement.

**Why SKYNZ?**

- ✅ **You own your page** (blockchain proof, can't be disputed)
- ✅ **No platform lock-in** (can sell/transfer your page)
- ✅ **Multiple revenue streams** (ads, badges, widgets, royalties)
- ✅ **Creator-friendly** (60% of page sales go to you)
- ✅ **Limited editions** (scarcity = value)
- ✅ **Live connection** (real-time chat with fans)
- ✅ **Fair pricing** (you set ad prices)

## Platform Features

### Creator Pages

**Three tiers of creator pages:**

| Tier | Price | With Logo | Features | Limit |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Basic** | $99.95 | $74.95 | Proof of ownership, NFT cert | Unlimited |
| **Pro** | $249.95 | $187.45 | Premium design, 5 ad slots, analytics | 100/month |
| **SuperModel** | $799.95 | $599.95 | Ultra-premium, 10 ad slots, rare | 10/month |

**What's included:**
- Live chat (real-time community connection)
- Creator analytics (page views, unique visitors, watch time)
- Music integration (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube)
- Ad space (you set prices)
- NFT certificate (blockchain proof of ownership)
- Collectible page (limited edition)

### Collectible Badges (For Fans)

Fans buy limited-edition collectible badges linked to creator pages, like digital trading cards. Badges are blockchain-verified, tradeable, and sellable on the marketplace.

### SKYNZ Owner Merch

All page owners get free SKYNZ merch. Rare merch giveaways create FOMO and drive page purchases. Creators wear merch in videos (free advertising for SKYNZ).

**Premium positioning** ($74.95-$599.95) reflects true value of creator ownership and blockchain verification.

## Getting Started

### For Developers

1. **Clone the repo**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/Justelliott6/SKYNZ.git
   cd SKYNZ
   ```

2. **Install dependencies**
   ```bash
   pnpm install
   ```

3. **Set up environment**
   ```bash
   cp .env.example .env.local
   ```

4. **Start development**
   ```bash
   pnpm dev
   ```

See [SETUP_GUIDE.md](./SETUP_GUIDE.md) for detailed instructions.

### For Creators

1. **Join the waitlist** (landing page)
2. **Get early access** (first 100 creators)
3. **Create your page** (choose tier)
4. **Earn revenue** (ads, badges, engagement)

## Key Differentiators

### 1. Creator Ownership
Creators own their pages (blockchain proof). Not rented. Not controlled by algorithms.

### 2. Premium Positioning
Premium pricing ($74.95-$599.95) attracts serious creators and reflects true value.

### 3. Multiple Revenue Streams
Page sales, ads, badges, widgets, royalties—creators have many ways to earn.

Logo discount incentive drives organic discovery. No marketing budget needed.

Scarcity creates value. Pages are collectible, not commodities.

60% of page sales go to creators. Fair revenue split.
