## Tips for Success

1. **Be authentic** - Share your real journey, not corporate speak
2. **Ask questions** - "What's YOUR biggest creator frustration?"
3. **Engage** - Reply to every comment, build community
4. **Use visuals** - Custom badges + video reels get 3x more engagement
5. **Tell stories** - The "8 months of code" narrative resonates
6. **Show progress** - "Still writing code" shows transparency
7. **Create urgency** - "Limited to first 100" drives action
8. **Link to demo** - Make it easy to see what you've built
9. **Vary content** - Mix features, story, urgency, behind-the-scenes
10. **Track results** - Monitor which posts drive most clicks/signups
